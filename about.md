# About Me

Hi, I am **Ranjeet Kumar** , a Senior Technical Writer with 11 years of experience in planning, authoring, testing , and offering product documentation at enterprise scale.

## What I Do

I specialize in creating user-centric documentation for enterprise software, with deep expertise in following skill sets.

- **API Documentation** - REST APIs, command references, developer guides
- **SaaS Documentation** - Cloud and on-premise solutions
- **User Guides** - Installation, configuration, and troubleshooting docs
- **Release Notes** - Features and bugs resolution
- **Doc-as-Code** - Modern documentation workflows using Git, Oxygen XML, and Markdown
- **Authoring Tool Used** - Oxygen XML, Adobe FrameWork, IXIASOFT CMS, and Confuence

## My Approach

I believe good documentation should have following properties:
- Be easy to search, navigate, and maintain
- Answer users' questions
- Use clear, concise language without jargon
- Include practical examples and relevant scenarios  
- Reduce support call with sales team and engineers.

I work closely with engineering, product, and UX teams to ensure documentation ships alongside product features.
